Name
Lie algebra
Notation
$\mathrm{Lie}$
Generating operation(s)
$[x,y]$ (bracket)
Symmetry properties
$[x,y]=-[y,x]$ (anti-symmetry)
Relations
$[[x,y],z]=[x,[y,z]]+[[x,z],y]$ (Leibniz relation)
$\dim\mathrm{Lie}(n)$
  1. 1
  2. 1
  3. 2
  4. 6
  5. 24
  6. 120
  7. 720
general term: $\dim\mathrm{Lie}(n)=(n-1)!$
Generating series
$f^{\mathrm{Lie}}(t)=-\log(1-t)$
Dual operad
$\mathrm{Lie}^!=\mathrm{Com}$
Properties
  • binary
  • quadratic
  • Koszul
Relationships
\begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \mathrm{Ass}\mathrm{-alg}&\to\mathrm{Lie}\mathrm{-alg} \\ \mathrm{Lie}\mathrm{-alg}&\to\mathrm{Leib}\mathrm{-alg} \\ \mathrm{preLie}\mathrm{-alg}&\to\mathrm{Lie}\mathrm{-alg} \\ \end{aligned} \end{equation}
Unit
no
Comment
One of the "three graces": $\mathrm{Lie}$, $\mathrm{Ass}$, $\mathrm{Com}$.
Named after Sophus Lie.
Integration: Lie groups.
Name
Pre-Lie algebra
Notation
$\mathrm{preLie}$
Generating operation(s)
$\{x,y\}$
Symmetry properties
none
Relations
$\{\{x,y\},z\}-\{x,\{y,z\}\}=\{\{x,z\}y\}-\{x,\{z,y\}\}$
$\dim\mathrm{preLie}(n)$
  1. 1
  2. 2
  3. 9
  4. 64
  5. 625
  6. 1296
  7. 117649
general term: $\dim\mathrm{preLie}(n)=n^{n-1}$
Generating series
$f^{\mathrm{preLie}}(t)=y$ where $y=t\exp(y)$
Dual operad
$\mathrm{preLie}^!=\mathrm{Perm}$
Properties
  • binary
  • quadratic
  • Koszul
Relationships
\begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \mathrm{preLie}\mathrm{-alg}&\to\mathrm{Lie}\mathrm{-alg}&:&[x,y]=\{x,y\}-\{y,x\} \\ \mathrm{Dend}\mathrm{-alg}&\to\mathrm{preLie}\mathrm{-alg}&:&\{x,y\}=x\prec y-y\prec x \\ \mathrm{Brace}\mathrm{-alg}&\to\mathrm{preLie}\mathrm{-alg}&:&\text{forgetful functor} \\ \end{aligned} \end{equation}
Unit
$\{1,x\}=x=\{x,1\}$
